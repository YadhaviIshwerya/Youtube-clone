  Every day, millions of people come to YouTube to be informed,

   inspired or just plain delighted. Over time, questions have come

   make up our community can rely on.

Our products and policies


   We want you to have the best possible experience while you're

  using YouTube. So, our products help you find what you're looking

   for and stay in control of what you see. Meanwhile, our policies

  explain what you can and cannot do while you're there, so that

   everyone plays by the same rules.
